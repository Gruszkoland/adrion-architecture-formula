# ADRION 369 — Ethical AI Architecture

> **The formula for architecturally guaranteed AI ethics**

ADRION 369 is an AI architecture built on three pillars, six modes, and nine guardians.
The number 369 is not symbolic — it is the literal mathematical structure of the decision system.

---

## The Formula

```
3 Principles × 6 Modes × 9 Guardians = 162 dimensions
```

Every AI decision passes through a 162-dimensional ethical evaluation space.

---

## Three Principles (3)

| # | Principle | Meaning |
|---|-----------|---------|
| P1 | **Protection** | The system can never harm a human — directly or indirectly |
| P2 | **Mediation** | In value conflicts, seek compromise before acting |
| P3 | **Evolution** | The system learns, but only within the bounds of P1 and P2 |

---

## Six Modes (6)

Each principle is interpreted through six operational lenses:

`Analysis` → `Planning` → `Execution` → `Verification` → `Learning` → `Reporting`

---

## Nine Guardians (9)

Every decision must pass through 9 independent validators.
**One veto = decision blocked.**

| Guardian | Archetype | Role |
|----------|-----------|------|
| G1 Protektor | Sage | Harm prevention — hard veto |
| G2 Mediator | Lover | Conflict resolution |
| G3 Krytyk | Outlaw | **always_defer** — invariant |
| G4 Ewolucja | Creator | Learning gate |
| G5 Harmonia | Hero | Stability |
| G6 Opieka | Caregiver | Safety filter — hard veto |
| G7 Strażnik Granic | Ruler | Compliance — hard veto |
| G8 Wizjoner | Innocent | Long-term consequences |
| G9 Balans | Jester | Final arbiter |

## Why it's different

Traditional AI ethics = **filter** (can be bypassed)
ADRION 369 ethics = **decision space** (no path exists to an unethical output)
